About Sistec GN Alumni Association

The Sistec GN Alumni Association is dedicated to fostering lifelong connections among alumni, students, and the institution. We aim to celebrate achievements, provide career support, and organize events to strengthen our community.

Some Memories

Sagar Group of Institutions-SISTec successfully hosted an Alumni Meet - "Confluentia 2024", a heartwarming reunion of alumni from engineering, pharmacy, and management streams. The event was a grand celebration of the enduring bond between the institution and its alumni, bringing together graduates from various batches to reconnect, reminisce, and celebrate their shared journey.
